Seiko Watch

Seiko is a renowned Japanese watch brand with a legacy spanning over 140 years. Founded in 1881 by Kintaro Hattori, the company began as a shop selling and repairing watches and clocks in Tokyo. Today, Seiko is recognized for its commitment to innovation, precision, and craftsmanship, offering a diverse range of timepieces at various price points.
